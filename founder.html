Meet the Founder

The vision and drive behind Call Mint.

Harsh Shikotra

Harsh Shikotra is a BBA student at IIM Bangalore, specializing in Digital Business and Entrepreneurship. His mission is to leverage his analytical and leadership skills to create innovative solutions. As a visionary, he is passionate about building products that solve real-world problems and contribute positively to society.

His strong academic background and active involvement in extracurricular activities have prepared him to make a meaningful impact. Through Call Mint, he aims to revolutionize mobile communication in India by creating a self-sustaining, ad-based ecosystem that provides free telecom services for all users.
